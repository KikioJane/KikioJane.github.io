Cyberpunk: Edgerunners Summary

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners is a 2022 anime based on the video game Cyberpunk 2077 that was released in 2020. The anime serves as a standalone prequle to the game with the events in Edgerunners taking place in the year 2076. The story focuses more on Cyberpsychosis and how it comes to effect people with cybernetic implants. For more information you can visit the Wikipedia page.
